Exporting India’s Agricultural Excellence Worldwide

How Indian Turmeric is Conquering Global Markets

Indian turmeric is renowned worldwide for its high curcumin content and quality. With increasing global awareness of turmeric’s medicinal and culinary benefits, exports are growing rapidly.

Indian exporters maintain strict quality standards, providing EU and FDA certifications, which help Indian turmeric stand out in international markets. Sustainability and traceability also play a major role in building trust with importers.

This growth has created new opportunities for farmers and exporters, strengthening India’s position as the leading global turmeric supplier.
