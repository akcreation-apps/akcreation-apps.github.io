Why Do You Need a Modified Grade Sheet?

Submitting the default printout from the BPUT result portal can hurt your first impression. Here’s why:

  • ✅ Backlog subjects often appear on a separate page in the portal printout.
  • ⚠️ That makes it obvious you cleared some papers in multiple attempts.

FAQs

📘 What is a Modified Grade Sheet?

A cleaned, consolidated grade sheet that reflects your correct, recomputed SGPA after clearing backlogs—without split pages or confusing duplicates.


📊 Why does BPUT show mismatched SGPAs?

Backlog clears often list on a separate page and the portal doesn’t re-run the weighted SGPA across all cleared credits—so both the “old” and “new” SGPAs can be wrong.


🧠 Is the calculation accurate?

Yes. The app uses the official BPUT formula: CGPA/SGPA = Σ(grade points × credits) ÷ Σ(credits), recomputed after clears.


📱 Is it available on the website?

No. This feature is exclusive to our Android app for a better, secure experience and offline access.
