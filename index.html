FAQs – BPUT CGPA, SGPA & Marks

Everything you need to know about BPUT grade calculations.

BPUT had no marks system until 2020. Each subject is out of 100, with 'O' grade mapped to 90–100. This app uses the official CGPA-to-percentage formula to estimate your secured marks.
Go to the 'Marks' section, enter your CGPA and number of subjects (or sum of known marks). The app instantly calculates your total and secured marks.
After clearance, updated SGPA isn't reflected automatically. Re-enter your grades in the SGPA utility, replacing 'F' with your actual cleared grade — that gives your corrected SGPA.
