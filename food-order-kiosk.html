How It Works

Customer Journey in 5 Steps

Frictionless ordering from scan to delivery

1
Scan the QR Code

Each table has a unique QR. Customer scans it with their phone camera — no app needed.

2
Instant Menu Access

A temporary session link opens the full menu in the browser — valid for 1 hour.

3
Browse & Place Order

Customer selects dishes, reviews the cart, and confirms the order.

4
WhatsApp Notification

The restaurant receives the order instantly on WhatsApp. No hardware required.

5
Delivery & Digital Bill

Once approved, the customer can view and download a digital bill directly on their phone.
