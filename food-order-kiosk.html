Customer Experience

  • 📌 Scan the QR Code: Customers scan a unique QR placed on the table.
  • 🔗 Get Instant Access: A temporary link opens the menu for 1 hour.
  • 🍔 Place an Order: Select food and confirm the order.
  • 📲 WhatsApp Order Notification: Restaurant receives orders via WhatsApp.
  • ✅ Food Delivery & Digital Bill: Customers can view and download their digital bill once the order is approved.

Admin Features & Control Panel
