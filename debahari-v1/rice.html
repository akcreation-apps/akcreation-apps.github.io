Understanding International Rice Quality Standards

Export-quality rice must meet strict international standards, including grain size, moisture content, purity, and absence of contaminants.

Certifications such as Phytosanitary Certificates, Certificates of Origin, and SGS reports ensure that the rice meets importer expectations and regulatory requirements.

Understanding these standards is crucial for exporters to maintain global trust and secure long-term business relationships.
