Core Partner Policies
Clause 1 — Packaging Standards

Premium Packaging is Mandatory

All food items prepared for delivery through BankiBites must be packed in premium, hygienic packaging. This is non-negotiable and directly impacts the customer experience and your restaurant's reputation on the platform.

  • Food must be packed in food-grade sealed containers (plastic or biodegradable) or wrapped securely in food-safe aluminium foil paper.
  • Loose packing in open packets, newspaper, or non-food-grade materials is strictly not permitted.
  • Liquid or semi-liquid items (gravies, soups, beverages) must use leak-proof containers with sealed lids.
  • Each package must be clearly identifiable — label or sticker with the item name is strongly recommended.
  • Packaging must ensure food remains fresh, hot, and intact throughout the delivery journey.
Clause 2 — Menu Availability

Keep BankiBites Updated on Stock

It is the restaurant partner's sole responsibility to proactively notify BankiBites about the real-time availability of every item on your menu. Customers rely on the listed menu to place their orders — any mismatch causes a poor experience and reflects on the entire platform.

  • If any item is temporarily unavailable (out of stock, seasonal, or preparation not possible), inform BankiBites before orders come in.
  • Notify us via WhatsApp or the agreed communication channel at the start of each business day.
  • If an item is permanently removed from your menu, update BankiBites immediately to delist it.
Penalty: If a customer orders an unavailable item and BankiBites was not notified, your restaurant will be delisted for 24 hours.

Repeated violations (3 or more within 30 days) may result in permanent removal from the BankiBites platform without appeal.

Clause 3 — Food Quality & Customer Satisfaction

Restaurant Bears Full Responsibility for Food Quality

BankiBites is a delivery platform — we connect customers to your restaurant. The quality, taste, hygiene, and safety of every food item prepared and packed is entirely the restaurant's responsibility.

  • In any case of customer dissatisfaction related to food quality, taste, hygiene, or incorrect items, the restaurant partner is fully responsible.
  • If a refund or replacement is issued to the customer due to a food-related complaint, the entire cost is borne by the restaurant. BankiBites will not absorb this loss.
  • BankiBites will not be held liable for food spoilage, contamination, allergic reactions, or any health issue arising from the food prepared by the restaurant.
  • Restaurants must maintain a clean, hygienic kitchen and comply with all applicable local food safety norms.
  • If a customer raises a valid complaint, BankiBites reserves the right to investigate and temporarily suspend the restaurant listing pending review.
General Conduct & Platform Rules
Clause 4 — General Obligations

Partner Code of Conduct

  • Restaurants must be ready to accept orders during their listed operating hours. Consistent unavailability during listed hours will result in review.
  • Food must be ready for pickup by the delivery partner within the committed preparation time. Delays repeatedly cause customer dissatisfaction.
  • Partners must treat BankiBites delivery staff and coordinators with respect at all times.
  • Any attempt to collect direct payments from BankiBites customers outside the platform is a breach of this agreement.
  • BankiBites reserves the right to update these guidelines. Partners will be notified of material changes at least 48 hours in advance.
