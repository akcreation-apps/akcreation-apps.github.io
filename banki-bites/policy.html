Core Policies
Clause 1 — Packaging Standards

Premium Packaging is Mandatory

  • All food must be packed in food-grade sealed containers or aluminium foil paper — no newspaper or open packets.
  • Liquids and gravies must use leak-proof containers with sealed lids.
  • Packaging must keep food fresh, hot, and intact throughout delivery.
Clause 2 — Menu Availability

Notify BankiBites of Any Unavailable Items

  • Inform BankiBites every morning about items you cannot prepare before orders are placed.
  • If an item is permanently removed from your menu, update BankiBites immediately to delist it.
Penalty: Failure to notify unavailability → restaurant delisted for 24 hours. 3+ violations in 30 days → permanent removal.
Clause 3 — Food Quality & Customer Satisfaction

Restaurant Bears Full Responsibility

  • All food quality, taste, hygiene, and safety is entirely the restaurant's responsibility. BankiBites is not liable.
  • Refunds or replacements due to food complaints are fully borne by the restaurant.
  • Maintain a clean, hygienic kitchen and comply with all local food safety norms at all times.
General Conduct
Clause 4 — Partner Code of Conduct

Platform Rules

  • Be ready to accept orders during your listed operating hours at all times.
  • Never collect direct payments from BankiBites customers outside the platform.
  • Treat BankiBites delivery staff and coordinators with respect.

Clause 1 — Availability Notification Mandatory

Inform BankiBites by 10:00 AM if Unavailable

  • Notify BankiBites before 10:00 AM every day you cannot work. No notification = you are considered on duty.
  • Any delivery assigned after 10:00 AM without prior notice is your full responsibility to complete.
Penalty: Abandoning an assigned delivery without prior notice → 48-hour suspension (first offence). Repeated violations → permanent removal.
